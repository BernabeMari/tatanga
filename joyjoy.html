ABAC Policies (Show/Hide)

Policy 1: Administrators have full access to all resources.

Policy 2: Users can only access resources owned by their department, unless they are managers or above.

Policy 3: User's security clearance must be equal to or higher than the resource classification.

Policy 4: Delete actions are restricted to resource owners or administrators.

Policy 5: Access to confidential or higher resources requires security training.

Policy 6: Access to top-secret resources is only allowed from corporate network and devices.

Policy 7: Financial records can only be accessed during business hours (9:00-17:00).
